PHASE 2
Pending

NFT Hybrid Economy

Setting the project ready for the upgrade to allow investors to stack
their $THJ holdings and adopt beehives in the virtual worlds. Every beehive
adopted produces one Honey NFT every month for its adopter’s wallet.
Adopters can give the hives to gamers to play and to earn Honey NFTs.
Both adopters and players receive each a Honey NFT/hive/month.
If the adopter also plays with his own hives and complete the challenges,
the hive generates extra rare Honey NFTs to be earned by the owner.
All the Honey NFTs are carriers of real honey and give the owner to
withdraw (once) to any physical address so they can enjoy the process of
bringing virtual assets to real world.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I receive real honey for stacking THJ Tokens?

Yes. We unite the digital with the real world by providing real pure honey made by the bees, for every hive our adopters are stacking THJ Tokens.
‍
Example: for stacking 50,000 THJ Tokens your reward is 1Kg of real honey every month.

Is there an annual maintenance fee?

Yes but optional. All the adopters can continue after the 2nd year of adoption with a small fee of only € 55 per year, ensuring to keep the hives protected and well taken car

e of, keeping their adopter privilege and continue to receive top quality honey.!

How much it costs to withdraw honey?

We allow you to use any shipping company you prefer (DHL, FEDEX, ANY), there is no problem with us! Their price usually depends on the volume and weight and the distance.
‍
Every adopter pays the shipping and packaging costs. Receiving the honey to your physical address depends on the packaging type and the shipping company of your choosing. We fulfill every order and give the goods to any courier/shipping company you send to pick it up.

Is the honey tested?

Yes, all our honey is tested in more than one laboratory on a constant basis, for a better quality check..
‍
We can also provide the medical and export certificates if requested when delivering the product to our THJ adopters. Our product is top quality, rare, pure and organic raw honey, directly from the hive..
